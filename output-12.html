Konusma Seviyeniz:
ne demek?
Bu, unlar yapabileceiniz anlamna gelir:
Read aloud a short text based on a common situation and pronounce familiar words and phrases quite clearly, and
generally use the correct stress, rhythm and intonation.
Answer a range of questions about everyday objects, places and activities that people do.
Describe people, places, possessions, daily routines and everyday activities that you can see in pictures and give your
opinion about what you can see in simple terms.
Tell a very short story using a range of words and phrases in simple grammatical structures.
ngilizcemi gelitirmek iin ne yapabilirim?
te baz neriler:
Focus on pronouncing more English sounds clearly and comfortably, using the right intonation and stress patterns in
words and sentences so what you say is easier to understand.
Learn more words, phrases and expressions to help you talk about more topics and a wider range of familiar
situations in greater detail.
Practise describing a range of different every situations and giving your opinion on what is happening in pictures.
Practise talking about sequences of events in stories and things that are happening or which have happened

Yazma Seviyeniz:
ne demek?
Bu, unlar yapabileceiniz anlamna gelir:
Spell a number of familiar words with the help of pictures.
Recognise and use the correct word order in some simple phrases.
Give information using simple phrases and sentences about you and other people in writing.
Write simple messages, cards or emails.
ngilizcemi gelitirmek iin ne yapabilirim?

Try to write short descriptions of familiar events and personal experiences, including things that happened in the
past.
